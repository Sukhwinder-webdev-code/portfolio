About Us-Taaj Avenue

"Where every flavour tells a story....!"

Our Story

Taaj Avenue was born out of a deep love for authentic Indian cuisine and a desire to bring people together through food. What started as a humble family kitchen in 2015 has grown into a vibrant space where locals find comfort and travelers discover new favorites. Our journey began with one simple idea — to serve food that not only satisfies hunger but also warms the heart. Every dish we create carries the soul of tradition, the richness of spices, and the care of home-cooked meals.

Our Philosophy

At Taaj Avenue, we believe food is more than just taste — it's emotion, memory, and culture. We carefully craft each plate using high-quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a generous helping of love. Whether it’s the creamy Paneer Butter Masala, the crisp Masala Dosa, or our signature Veg Thali, we make sure every bite reflects our passion for flavor and hospitality.

Meet The People Behind the Plates

We’re a team of passionate chefs, friendly staff, and food lovers who believe that hospitality is just as important as taste. Our founder,Sukhwinder Kaur, started Taaj Avenue to share her grandmother’s recipes with the world — and now we serve thousands with that same warmth and tradition.
